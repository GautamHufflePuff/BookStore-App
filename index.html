Harry Potter
Harry Potter and the Philospher's Stone, also known as the Sorcerer's Stone is J.K Rowling's first and most precious book!

The first novel in the Harry Potter series and Rowling's debut novel, it follows Harry Potter, a young wizard who discovers his magical heritage on his eleventh birthday, when he receives a letter of acceptance to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. This book has never failed to "wow!" me again and again no matter how many times I flip htrough the pages over and over. This is "the" book that started the wizarding world, and so, it is my very favorite, and a precious belonging. I had read this book about 3 years back, from my mother's copy which she bought when she was in 7th grade, and I read it so much the dusty pages of the novel looked brand new! Anyway, I highly recommend you read this book if you haven't already, trust me, it will bring a smile to your face!
